What is LifeMaxxing

Your life, beautifully on record.

Movies have IMDb. Music has charts. Your actual life deserves the same: the hikes, the dinners, the cities, all in one place you will actually want to open.

Log every experience

Places, ratings, photos, the story of the moment. Capture it in seconds and keep it forever. Your memories become a collection, not a camera roll graveyard.

  • Rate places out of 5. Your reviews, your record
  • Attach photos and the story behind them
  • Tag the activity: dining, adventure, wellness, travel

Discover what to do next

Never ask "so what should we do?" again. Explore experiences ranked by people you actually trust: trending, highest rated, near you, or picked by friends.

  • Filter by category, distance and rating
  • Friends' picks beat anonymous five star strangers
  • From your street to the other side of the world

Watch your Life Map fill up

Every log drops a pin. Zoom out and your life becomes a map: the cafes on your block, the summits on your bucket list, and every country in between.

  • Your personal atlas of everywhere you have been
  • Places, cities and countries, counted for you
  • See friends' pins layered over yours

Every place has a page.

A restaurant in Lisbon isn't a photo that vanishes from your feed. It's a permanent node in the Life Graph — every rating, every visit, every person who went there. Structured like IMDb, built for real life.

  • Person, place, activity, date, rating — all connected
  • Places ranked by people you actually trust
  • The more you live, the smarter it gets
